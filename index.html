🎯 Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

The principles and goals that guide everything we do at CBST

🔭

Our Vision

To emerge as a centre of excellence in Science & Technology education, fostering innovation, ethics, and lifelong learning for the betterment of society.

🚀

Our Mission

  • Provide quality undergraduate education in Science & Technology
  • Develop holistic personalities through academics and co-curricular activities
  • Promote research aptitude and scientific temper among students
  • Ensure inclusive education with equal opportunities for all
  • Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements

Admission Notice PDF Admission Procedure

How to Apply

  1. Obtain the Prospectus & Application Form from the college office or download online
  2. Fill the application form with correct personal & academic details
  3. Attach required documents (mark sheets, certificates, photos)
  4. Submit the form along with the application fee at the college office
  5. Attend the admission counselling / merit-based selection process
  6. Pay the semester fee and complete college enrollment
